Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our catering services

How far in advance should I book your catering services?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for most events. For large events or peak seasons (holidays, wedding season), we suggest booking 6-8 weeks ahead to ensure availability.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Absolutely! We can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other specific dietary requirements. Please inform us of any allergies or restrictions when booking.

What's included in your catering packages?

Our packages typically include food preparation, serving staff, basic serving equipment, and cleanup. We can also provide additional services like decorations, equipment rental, and event coordination for an additional fee.

Do you provide serving staff for events?

Yes, we provide professional serving staff for all our catering events. Our team is trained to deliver exceptional service and ensure your guests have a wonderful dining experience.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Osogbo metropolitan area and surrounding regions. For events outside our standard service area, we may charge additional delivery fees. Contact us to discuss your specific location.
